The administrator accesses the platform and authenticates in it.
The platform asks what action the administrator intends to perform (Register, Consult, Edit, Delete).
The administrator chooses to register a parking space. The platform asks the administrator to fill the required data (single designation, floor, price, parking lot name).
The administrator inputs the requested data.
The platform shows the data to the administrator asking him to confirm it.
